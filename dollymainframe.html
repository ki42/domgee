Production - Dolly Grip
When Megan was 20, she first laid eyes on a Chapman. As Video Village, she ended up wrangling the cable a lot of times for the dolly moves.

Megan thought it would be awesome to be the Dolly Grip, so she took every opportunity to do it.

Though she operated several times, Megan wasn't being given the title in the credits, so she actually made it a goal to get the title.

In 2006, she worked on "Dream Slashers" and did every dolly shot in the film except for one.
