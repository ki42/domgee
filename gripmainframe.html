Production - Grip

For those of you that don't know, Grips are the problem solvers on set. Anything that is off camera and doesn't plug in is what Grips are responsible for.

That includes shaping the light, putting down wood so everyone can walk over the swamp to get to set, and putting the camera in all sorts of positions attached in all different ways.

Megan has been Griping since 2003.
