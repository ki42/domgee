Post Production - Video Editor
Megan started editing with Avid Symphony in her high school in 1999.

During college, she interned at the Capital Children's Museum in Washington D.C. and edited linerly on 3/4in and Beta machines.

In college, she learned FCP 2, 4, and 5 as the versions were released. She used college equipment to edit over 200 hours of footage from various projects.

She was the editor of "Hometown Girls" a thirty minute, three episode news magazine show, and "Restoring the Past, Building the Future" a 12 minute documentary for the Missouri Theater.

She was a part of the Los Angeles Final Cut Pro Users Group.
