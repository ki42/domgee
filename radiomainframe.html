Voice Talent - Radio DJ

Megan started working in radio in 2002. She worked for KKPL FM (what was then Planet Radio) as a promotions intern and KWWC FM as a DJ/Producer.

On KWWC, Megan had two shows, New Age Sundae, and Momentum Mondays. These shows did not overlap, with New Age Sundae ending in 2003 and Momentum Mondays starting in 2004.

As part of her shows, Megan did weather, news, and sports broadcasts. She also produced a few PSAs and Liners.
